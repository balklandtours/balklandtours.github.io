Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to add nofollow?

No. Regular HTML links are fine. Do not add rel="nofollow" unless you specifically want that.

Should index.html be in the same folder?

Yes. Put index.html in the same root folder as: balkan-tours.html, balkan-travel.html, unesco-balkans.html, and sarajevo-travel.html.

Can I add more pages later?

Yes. You can expand this structure with pages like mostar-travel.html, bosnia-tours.html, or balkan-itinerary.html.
